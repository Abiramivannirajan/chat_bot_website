Job Application Status Tracking

Tracking the status of your job applications is essential for managing your career progress effectively.

Why Use This System?

This system helps you categorize job applications into different statuses— Favorite, Applied, Joined, and Rejected—allowing you to stay organized.

How It Works

By dragging and dropping job entries, you can easily update their statuses as you progress through the hiring process. Whether you're shortlisting a job, awaiting a response, or have successfully joined a company, this visual tracking system ensures you never lose track of your job search journey.
